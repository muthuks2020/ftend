About Us

Explore your city with The University Paper as we give you the best places to eat, drink and make the most of uni life.

Sounds great but how do we do it?

Well, we’ve got hundreds of student writers across the UK eager to share their unique knowledge of the amazing restaurants, clubs and more going on in their city.

Our team of professional journalists give advice to the student writers on how to make every article sparkle with witty and engaging copy.

So whether you want the best burgers in Bristol or the best places for a Tinder date in Edinburgh, we’ve got it covered.

And (although we now pull in hundreds of thousands of readers a month) it all started on a much smaller scale at Nottingham Trent University in 2013.

The University Paper was founded as a free newspaper by student Chris Moss, covering news stories on Trent and its student population.

There were only 10,000 copies per edition but it quickly became one of the most exciting brands in the city and was embraced by the student population.

Following its success, the paper was expanded to cover all the Nottingham universities and then Bristol’s.

After moving our HQ to London, we now cover 16 cities across the UK, pulling in hundreds of thousands of readers a month, who all come to us for advice on their local cities and our gripping and informative articles.
